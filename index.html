Clive & The Chaos Crew

Wrestling. Romance chaos. Rivalries. Party Hips energy.

Clive is an 18-year-old wrestling champ standing 6’2 with a chaotic romantic life. He’s surrounded by friends who all want him in different ways, while he tries to figure out who he loves.


Peter is a tiny but intense hardworking guy who struggles to express emotions. Russel writes music about Clive. Chris is a shy rival who competes emotionally with Alex. Alex is a swimmer obsessed with Clive’s attention. Holden is the calm one stuck in the middle of everyone’s chaos.


Tucker plays Roblox anime games, Hamster Hemmit watches Clive from afar, James is chaotic and bitter, and Hans Vermhit is Clive’s hat-selling rival.


After emotional chaos and a house party meltdown, Clive finally chooses Peter. Peter admits he has loved Clive for months, and the rest of the group slowly accepts it.

The Full Story

Clive was an 18-year-old wrestling champ standing 6’2 with broad shoulders, powerful thighs, and an easy smile that made half the guys at school weak in the knees. He was openly gay and surrounded by a chaotic crew of friends who all, in their own strange ways, wanted him. Choosing just one felt impossible.


Peter was the 5’1 firecracker—stunningly hot with sharp cheekbones and intense eyes, a strange mix of incel awkwardness and hardworking Chad energy. He busted his ass at two jobs but could barely say “I like you” without turning red and changing the subject. Russel, the flirty twink with headphones always around his neck, would blast indie music and write little songs about Clive’s wrestling matches. Chris, the tiny 4’9 shy boy, burned with quiet longing for Clive and clashed with Alex, his “love rival.”


Tucker, Hamster Hemmit, James, Alex, Holden, and Hans Vermhit all orbit Clive’s life in chaotic ways—rivalries, obsession, friendship, and conflict constantly overlapping.


One night after a house party filled with tension, music, and emotional breakdowns, Clive finally admits he can’t choose everyone. He chooses Peter.


Peter, shocked, confesses he’s loved Clive for months. The group reacts with chaos, acceptance, jealousy, and music. But eventually things settle.


Clive and Peter stay together, growing into a stable relationship while the rest of the group continues their chaotic but loyal friendship.
