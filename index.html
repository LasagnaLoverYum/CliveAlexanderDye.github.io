The Full Story

Clive was an 18-year-old wrestling champ standing 6’2 with broad shoulders, powerful thighs, and an easy smile that made half the guys at school weak in the knees. He was openly gay and surrounded by a chaotic crew of friends who all, in their own strange ways, wanted him.


Peter was the 5’1 firecracker—stunningly hot with sharp cheekbones and intense eyes, a hardworking Chad who struggled to express feelings. Russel was the flirty twink musician writing songs about Clive’s matches. Chris, the 4’9 shy boy, burned with jealousy toward both Alex and Holden.


Alex was the 5’6 swimmer obsessed with seeing Clive do Party Hips in swim briefs, constantly clashing with Chris. Holden was calmer, more grounded, often caught in the middle of everyone’s chaos.


Tucker played anime Roblox games, Hamster Hemmit watched Clive from afar, James was bitter and chaotic, and Hans Vermhit remained Clive’s strange hat-selling rival.


After weeks of emotional chaos, wrestling matches, pool parties, and arguments, Clive finally chose Peter after a house party breakdown. Peter, shocked but overwhelmed, admitted he’d loved Clive for months.


From then on, Clive and Peter stayed together—Clive the strong wrestler, Peter the intense but loving smaller partner who finally learned how to express himself.


The crew stayed chaotic, but stable. Even Hans eventually sold matching hats. Somehow, it all worked out.
