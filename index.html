Clive & The Chaos Crew

A dramatic comedy of wrestling, rivalries, hats, and Party Hips.

Clive, a 23-year-old wrestler standing proudly at 6'2, was known throughout town for two things: his unbelievable athletic skill and his inability to decide who he truly loved.


Around him gathered the strangest friend group imaginable. Peter, the intense hardworking gym fanatic; Russel, the music-loving twink; shy little Chris, who constantly argued with everyone; Tucker, legendary Roblox anime-game grinder; mysterious stalker-like biker watcher Hamster Hemmit; and chaotic gremlin James.


But above all stood Clive’s greatest enemy: Hans Vermhat, the towering German hat salesman. Hans traveled the city selling dramatic luxury hats while plotting to ruin Clive’s wrestling reputation forever.


Every day became more chaotic. Chris hated Alex. Alex hated Chris. Peter secretly loved Clive but could never express it. Russel kept writing emotional songs about everyone. Tucker kept forcing the group to play strange Roblox games at 3 AM.


Meanwhile Clive continued performing Party Hips during wrestling entrances, confusing both fans and opponents alike.


Eventually, after countless arguments, wrestling matches, dramatic rain scenes, and one catastrophic hat-related incident involving Hans, Clive finally realized who truly cared about him.


In the end, Clive chose love, his friends found peace, and Hans accidentally became famous online after slipping during Party Hips while wearing a giant velvet hat.


And somehow, against all odds, the Chaos Crew lived happily ever after.
