Clive & The Chaos Crew

Wrestling. Rivalries. Hats. Party Hips. A completely unhinged romance adventure.

The Story

Clive, a 23-year-old wrestler standing proudly at 6'2, was famous throughout the city for his athletic talent, chaotic confidence, and his dramatic Fortnite Party Hips dance entrances.


Yet beneath the confidence, Clive struggled with one impossible problem: he had too many confusing feelings for too many people.


His friend group was complete chaos. Peter, the intense 5'1 hardworking gym chad, secretly cared deeply for Clive but could barely express emotion. Russel constantly made emotional playlists and dramatic music edits. Chris, a shy 4'9 rival, desperately wanted Clive’s attention while constantly fighting with Alex.


Alex, standing at 5'6, loved swimming more than anything. He constantly invited Clive to pools and beaches, mostly because he enjoyed watching Clive perform Party Hips in ridiculous swim outfits. Chris considered Alex his greatest romantic rival, and the two argued almost daily.


Meanwhile, Holden was the calm one of the group. Cool-headed and stylish, Holden often acted as the voice of reason whenever the others became completely insane. Unfortunately, Chris disliked Holden because of the strange people Holden associated with, especially Hans Vermhat.


Then there was Tucker, legendary Roblox anime-game player and professional online debater. Hamster Hemmit, the mysterious bike-watching stalker, and James, the chaotic gremlin nobody trusted around computers.


Above them all stood Clive’s greatest enemy: Hans Vermhat, the massive German hat salesman. Hans sold luxury hats across the city while plotting to destroy Clive’s wrestling reputation forever.


One evening everything exploded into chaos during the annual Wrestling Festival. Chris and Alex began screaming at each other beside the pool, Tucker lost a Roblox tournament, Hans attempted to sell giant velvet hats to random wrestlers, and Clive accidentally performed Party Hips in front of thousands of people.


Somehow, through all the chaos, Clive finally realized who truly cared for him. After late-night talks, emotional arguments, and one catastrophic hat accident involving Hans, Clive made his choice.


In the end, the group slowly stopped fighting, Hans accidentally became internet famous, Russel wrote a song about the entire disaster, and Clive finally found happiness with the one person who understood him best.


And thus, the Chaos Crew lived happily ever after.
